## 🔬 Literature Review Table

| Evaluation Field | Researcher Analysis |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Problem Addressed** <br>*(1-2 lines)* | _Translating massive, complex omics (genomics and phenomics) data into practical trait predictions and gene function discoveries remains a significant bottleneck to keep up with the increasing requirement fro crop breeding._ |
| **Methodology & Approach** <br>*(2-3 lines)* | _The paper reviews the integration of next-generation sequencing and high-throughput phenotyping using diverse machine learning (ML) algorithms to manage big data and build predictive models. It also presents a case study applying seven distinct ML approaches  via the MLseq platform to identify microRNAs (miRNAs) associated with cold stress tolerance in wild potato clones._ |
| **Key Results & Findings** <br>*(2-3 lines)* | _ML algorithms demonstrate high capability in processing large agricultural datasets to predict genomic estimated breeding values and identify functional traits. In the specific case study, the applied ML models correctly classified 100% of the test samples into tolerant or susceptible genotypes and identified 52 miRNAs associated with cold tolerance, 25 of which were novel predictions._ |
| **Limitations Identified** <br>*(1-2 lines)* | _Developing robust ML models requires large, expensive, and time-consuming datasets, and these models often lack universal applicability across different crop species or fluctuating real-world environmental conditions without extensive validation._ |
| **Future Scope** <br>*(2-3 lines)* | _Future research expects ML models to be widely applied across various omics disciplines to integrate data and resolve key biological questions. This expansion will necessitate enhanced computational infrastructures, improved data-sharing practices, and deep-learning approaches capable of comparative genomics across multiple species._ |
| **Project Relevance** <br>*(1-2 lines)* | _Integrating ML algorithms with massive genomic data provides a highly relevant framework for our genome sequencing project to automate data mining, identify target genes, and build robust predictive models for complex traits._ |
